## Sample Full-Day Private Everest Helicopter Experience

Below is a potential outline of a daylong private Everest helicopter tour. You can modify it based on your own interests.

### **Early Morning Start**
- Hotel pickup around **5:30 AM**
- Meet your pilot at a **private helipad** at Tribhuvan Airport  
- Enjoy a quick safety briefing and coffee  
- Take off around **6 AM** in an AS350 or similar helicopter  

As Kathmandu fades below, discuss your preferences—extra passes, slow hovers, or special stops.

---

### **First Leg: Kathmandu to Lukla**
- **40-minute flight** to Lukla, the famous gateway to Everest  
- Hover low over the **Dudh Koshi River**  
- Spot trekkers crossing suspension bridges  
- Refuel in Lukla, grab a quick pastry if you’d like  

Continue deeper into the **Solukhumbu region**, with a pass over Tengboche Monastery around **7 AM**. Request a circle for perfect monastery-and-forest photos.

### **Everest Panorama Flight**
Around **7:30 AM**, the star moment arrives.  
- Orbit around **Everest**, **Lhotse**, **Nuptse**, and the **Khumbu Icefall**  
- Optional detours toward **Cho Oyu** or **Makalu**  

Land at **Kala Patthar (5,545 m)** around **8 AM** for the best morning light.

### **Kala Patthar Landing**
- Spend **20–30 minutes** here  
- Capture iconic photos  
- Toast with champagne if pre-arranged  
- Enjoy the silence and expansive views  

---

### **Everest Base Camp & Breakfast Stop**
If it’s climbing season (April–May):
- Hover above **Everest Base Camp**  
- Land nearby to observe expedition activity  

Around **9:30 AM**, fly to **Syangboche** or **Kongde Lodge** for a **private breakfast** overlooking **Ama Dablam**.

---

## Afternoon Options
Choose one or more optional add-ons:

- Fly over the **Gokyo Lakes**  
- Stop at **Namche Bazaar** for Sherpa culture  
- Brief **yoga session** at altitude  
- Extra scenic loops around hidden valleys  

Begin your return flight to Kathmandu around **1 PM**, possibly passing over **Bhote Koshi River** or cliffside monasteries.

Land by **2 PM**, giving you the rest of the afternoon free.

## Planning Your Personalized Helicopter Adventure

- Choose a reputable operator (e.g., Himalayan Helicopters)  
- Book **1–2 months** ahead during peak seasons  
- Share your exact preferences: romance, adventure, photography, wellness  
- Costs typically include permits, fuel, and meals  

### Health & Preparation
- Prepare for altitude—consult a doctor if needed  
- Acclimate in Kathmandu first  
- Pack layers, sunglasses, sunscreen, and a camera  
- Respect the **15 kg weight limit**  
- Weather may require flexible scheduling
